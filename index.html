Disaster Recovery Preparedness: Are You Ready?

Over half of those who responded to a recent survey indicated that their Disaster Recovery (DR) plan didn’t provide a reliable safety net when the worst actually happened. Neglecting to conduct a thorough test of your DR plan before an emergency occurs can leave your company vulnerable to an IT crisis. Expedient’s 6 Steps to Disaster Recovery Preparedness, a free, downloadable PDF, can serve as a basis for your organization’s approach to data protection so that your organization can rebound quickly after disaster strikes.
